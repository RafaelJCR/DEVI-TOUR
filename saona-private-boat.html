Private excursions are fully customizable. You can choose your preferred departure time, return time, and itinerary. The experience is 100% flexible and personalized. Prices vary depending on the number of people in the group.

About this Excursion ESTO NO ES VERIFDICO

The Saona Island excursion begins when we pick you up directly from your hotel. From there we travel to the beautiful fishing village of Bayahibe where the adventure starts.

Depending on the departure schedule, you will travel either by speed boat or by catamaran. If we depart by speed boat, we will first stop at the famous Natural Pool for about 25–35 minutes where you can enjoy the shallow crystal-clear waters.

After the stop, we continue to Saona Island where you will spend the rest of the day relaxing on the beach, swimming in turquoise waters, and enjoying the tropical scenery.

The return trip is done on the opposite boat type — if you went by speed boat you will return by catamaran, and vice versa. During the catamaran ride you will enjoy music, entertainment and drinks on board.

The excursion includes:

  • Round-trip transportation
  • Professional tour guide
  • Entertainment on the boat
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Water
  • Soft drinks
  • Buffet style lunch

Deposit via PayPal. Remaining balance paid on excursion day.
