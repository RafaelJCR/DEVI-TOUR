Saona Island & Altos de Chavón

About this Excursion ESTO NO ES VERIDICO

Discover one of the most complete excursions in Punta Cana combining paradise beaches with Dominican history and culture.

Our adventure begins with transportation from your hotel to La Romana where we will visit the famous Mediterranean-style village of Altos de Chavón.

Altos de Chavón is a stunning replica of a 16th century Mediterranean village located above the Chavón River. Here you can explore its stone streets, art galleries and breathtaking viewpoints.

After Altos de Chavón we continue to:

  • Bayahibe village
  • Natural swimming pool in the Caribbean Sea
  • Saona Island paradise beaches

Enjoy a relaxing day with turquoise waters, white sand beaches, Dominican buffet lunch and tropical drinks while discovering one of the most beautiful islands in the Caribbean.
