Catalina Island & Altos de Chavón

About this Excursion ESTO NO ES VERIDICO

This excursion combines the beauty of Catalina Island with a cultural visit to the famous village of Altos de Chavón. The day begins with hotel pickup and transportation to La Romana where the adventure starts.

Our first stop is Altos de Chavón, a stunning Mediterranean-style village located above the Chavón River. Here you will have time to walk through its stone streets, visit artisan shops, and enjoy breathtaking views.

After visiting Altos de Chavón we continue to the port where the boat will take us to Catalina Island, one of the most beautiful snorkeling destinations in the Dominican Republic.

Once on Catalina Island you can relax on white sand beaches, swim in turquoise waters, and enjoy the tropical atmosphere of the Caribbean.

The excursion includes:

  • Round-trip transportation
  • Professional tour guide
  • Snorkeling equipment
  • Open bar with drinks
  • Water and soft drinks
  • Dominican buffet lunch
  • Beach time on Catalina Island
