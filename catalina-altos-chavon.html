Catalina Island & Altos de Chavón

About this Excursion

Explore Punta Cana’s surrounding landscape by land and water during a 9-hour tour. Step back in time with a visit to Altos de Chavón, a replica of a medieval Mediterranean village. Hop aboard a double-decker boat for a cruise along the Chavón River, then hop on a catamaran for a sailing adventure to Catalina Island. While on the island, enjoy a buffet lunch and use the provided diving equipment to explore the underwater world. Round-trip transportation from select Punta Cana hotels available.

Included services:

  • Alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks
  • Buffet lunch and snacks
  • Professional guide
  • Hotel pickup and drop-off
  • Vehicle with air conditioning
  • snorkeling equipment
