Saona Island From Santo Domingo

About this Excursion

This excursion begins in the city of Santo Domingo, after we leave Santo Domingo we will head to Bayahibe. There we will board a speed boat or a catamaran. If we go out by speedboat, we will make a stop in the morning at the natural pool for about 25 to 35 minutes, then we will go to Saona Island where we will spend the entire day and on the way back we will return on a catamran. If we board the motomaran in the morning, the stop at the natural pool will be in the afternoon.

The excursion includes:

  • Round trip transportation
  • Buffet style lunch
  • Professional tourist guide
  • Transportation
  • maritime by speedboat and catamran
  • Stop at the natural pool for 25 to 35 minutes
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • water and sodas
