Samaná, Whales & Cayo Levantado

About this Excursion

Take a guided day trip from Punta Cana to explore the eastern coastal region of the Dominican Republic. The tour begins with transportation from your hotel and a boat ride from Las Cañitas Pier to Samaná. From there, a safari-style truck takes you through the countryside to a ranch. At the ranch, you will ride a horse for about 40 minutes to reach El Limón Waterfall, one of the most impressive waterfalls in the Caribbean with a 67-meter drop. After spending time enjoying the waterfall, you return to the ranch for a traditional Dominican lunch. The tour continues with a visit to Cayo Levantado, also known as Isla Bacardí, where you can relax on white-sand beaches, swim in crystal-clear waters, or rest under coconut trees. After about an hour and a half on the island, the trip returns by boat to Las Cañitas.


what will you do?

  • Ride a horse for about 40 minutes one way to visit La Cascada Limón
