Santo Domingo City Tour

About this Excursion

After picking you up from your hotel, we travel to the historic capital city of Santo Domingo.

Our first stop will be the famous Los Tres Ojos National Park, a natural cave system with beautiful underground lagoons.

After that we will visit some of the most important historical landmarks of the city such as:

  • The First Cathedral of America
  • The Columbus Lighthouse
  • The Colonial Zone
  • The Santo Domingo Malecon
  • The Presidential Palace
  • The National Museum
  • Souvenir shops and local cultural spots

The excursion includes:

  • Entrance tickets to parks and museums
  • Round-trip transportation
  • Buffet style lunch
  • Non-alcoholic beverages
  • Professional tour guide
