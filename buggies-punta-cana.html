Buggies Adventure – Punta Cana

About this Excursion

The Buggies excursion operates in three daily schedules lasting approximately 3 hours.

After pickup from your hotel we drive to the ranch where safety instructions will be provided.

During the tour we visit a traditional Dominican ranch to see handmade local products.

We then go to Macao Beach where you can swim and wash off the mud.

The next stop is the famous Taino Cenote cave before returning to the ranch.

The excursion includes:

  • Round-trip transportation
  • Professional tour guide
  • Off-road buggy adventure
  • Visit Dominican ranch
  • Macao Beach stop
  • Taino Cenote cave
