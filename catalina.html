Catalina Island & Snorkeling

About this Excursion

After picking you up from your hotel, we will head to the city of La Romana. There we will board a motomaran, a boat that is smaller than a catamaran but faster and perfect for this adventure.

Once on board, we will sail to the coral reef where we will enjoy an incredible snorkeling experience for approximately 25 to 35 minutes in the crystal clear Caribbean waters.

After snorkeling, we will continue our journey to the beautiful Catalina Island, where you will spend the rest of the day relaxing, swimming and enjoying the beach.

The excursion includes:

  • Round-trip transportation
  • Professional tour guide
  • Buffet style lunch
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Entertainment and music
  • Snorkeling equipment
