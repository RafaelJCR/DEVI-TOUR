Catalina Island & Diving

About this Excursion

This excursion is available for adults only.

After picking you up from your hotel, we will head to the city of La Romana. There we will board a motomaran, a smaller but faster boat than a traditional catamaran.

Once on board, we will travel to the coral reef where we will complete two diving immersions, each lasting approximately one hour, exploring the beautiful underwater world of the Caribbean.

The excursion includes:

  • Two oxygen tanks and full diving equipment
  • Professional diving guide
  • Round-trip transportation
  • Buffet style lunch
  • Non-alcoholic beverages

If you do not have a diving license, an instructor can be assigned for an additional cost of $25 USD per person.
